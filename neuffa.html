Neuffa | Skin Sanctuary

Neuffa is an e-commerce platform offering beauty and skin care products from other countries be readily accessible to Indonesian market.

Details

Target Market:
20 - 30 year old

The Challenge:
To create a responsive e-commerce website for beauty and skincare products
that will appeal to both men and women.

Outcome:
A responsive website with a beautiful interface design with seamless integration of secure payment and delivery system.

Length:
8 weeks to develop initial design, 8 more to work parallel with developer team.

Role:
Lead UI Designer (research, visual design, user experience in mind)

Research

I did an extensive research of market competitors both inside and outside of Indonesia. Defining the standards for online shopping markets and figuring out how to make them even better.

Research Goals

  1. Define the client’s needs, business goals, and Brand Indentity.
  2. Understand what factors are needed to create a strong and reliable shopping platform for users.
  3. Looking at design trends for e-commerce web platforms that can be applied.
  4. Understanding user behaviour and facilitating the best online shopping experience.

Primary Research:
Client's Specification

The first phase of research was to have a sit-down session with the client and getting to know his business goals, what he likes and what he absolutely hates. The client was very specific from the very beginning, as I begin to create the user flow.

Secondary Research:
Market Competitors

the secondary phase of research was to understand and determine the standards of the beauty department of e-commerce businesses, and how to improve and deliver better results. I narrowed down to 5 leading beauty e-commerce websites at the time to the following:

Sociolla.com
Hermo.com
Benscrub.com
Sephora.com
Skinstore.com

Visual Design

Yay! the fun part!
I consulted with Henry, Neuffa’s Brand Identity Designer to create a whole Interface design
that is in line with the branding identity and guidelines.
I begin by creating a moodboard and then a rough initial sketches of how each page is going to look like. After a few pages, I continued digitally on Sketch. and switching back and forth for other pages.
After 2 weeks in, I worked in parallel sprints with the development team of two front end coders.

Design System

After designing several pages digitally and getting fine tune adjustments from both the client and identity designer, I proceed to create the UI kit, consisting of components such buttons, states, color palette, text styles, and pixel perfect icons.

Final Design

and finally, this is the finished look of Neuffa’s website!
