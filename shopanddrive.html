Shop & Drive | Automotives Responsive Website

Shop & Drive is a workshop Outlet for automotive spare parts. As part of Astra Group, Shop & Drive is one of the country’s leading autoshop.

Details

Target Market:
20 - 40 year old

The Challenge:
Redesigning Shop&Drive’s old website and turn it into a professional looking website as the digital presence of Shop & Drive as a global business enterprise.

Outcome:
A responsive website with a professional and clean design.

Length:
8 weeks to develop initial design, 8 more to work parallel with developer team.

Role:
UI Designer (research, visual design, design system) at VOIA Studio, working alongside a Creative Director, and a programmer to make our vision a reality!

Research

Since this is a redesigning project, we have already known most of the contents that will go on the new website will inherit the old one. But as a new addition, the client wanted a Comparison Feature. This feature would allow visitors to compare one product to the other (up to 5 products at once) to see the advantages of each one, and then make the decision to buy it later.

Research Goals

  1. Understanding what makes a comparison page effective and useful.
  2. Understanding the user behaviour and anticipate their actions for navigating the website.
  3. Defining the standard for automotive brands websites through design trend and user experience

So I did a comprehensive research of how comparisons pages are usually made, and what the market’s competitors look like. We really liked how Apple is doing with its comparison page, and also checked some other sites as well.

Secondary Research:
User Behaviour & Flow

The second phase of research was to understand the user flow and user behaviour while navigating through the website, especially for comparison as it is the main feature we’re going to add. The client didn’t want a buying and payment feature for now, so the endgame is to call services if the user decides they want to buy or know more about the product.

Tertiary Reasearch:
Market Competitors

Next, I needed to find and determine the standards for automotive websites, as well as retail online shops. The typical look and feel, and figure out how to improve and deliver better design and experience.

Wireframing

With the information gathered from the first three initial researches, I proceeded to create the first high-fidelity wireframes. and created the sitemap.

Visual Design

fter determining and creating the User flow and wireframes, I move on to developing the key visuals and exploring the design on Sketch.

Design System

After designing several pages digitally and getting fine tune adjustments from both the Client and Creative Director, I proceed to create the UI kit, consisting of components such as buttons, states, color palette, text styles, and pixel perfect icons. These are all then passed on to the Programmer, and he develops the website.

Final Design

and finally, we have the finished look of Shop & Drive’s website!
