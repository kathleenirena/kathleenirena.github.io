DeeMoney | Thai Based Banking App Case Study

DeeMoney is a Thailand Banking App that supports both local and international banking fund transfers and makes it easy and accessible to Thais and expatriates alike.

Details

Target Market:
20 - 30 year old

The Challenge:
To create a banking app that is fun to use and innovative, trusted, and reliable.

Outcome:
A responsive web application with a fun interface with seamless integration of banking system that still complies with Thailand regulatory requirements.

Length:
4 weeks

Role:
UI Designer (research, visual design, design system), Working with Creative Director.

Research

The nature of this project is working with a Creative Director to create the visual look and style based on an already existingwireframe that he had created prior.

Research Goals

So moving on from the wireframes, I still need to do a research of what the market’s competitors look like, and how the standards for Banking apps are set in order to deliver an effective and efficient product, essentially these are:

  1. Understanding the client’s needs, business goals, and Brand Indentity.
  2. Understanding what factors came into play to create a strong and reliable application that handles money.
  3. Researching market competitors and design trends to determine design style and direction.
  4. Understanding user behaviour and facilitating the best banking experience.

Primary Research:
Client Insight

Tthe first phase of research was to find information and understanding about the Client, their goals, and their needs. They wanted a design that looks unique, fun and easy to use, and not necessarily so serious and too corporate.

Secondary Reasearch:
Market Competitors

The second phase of research was to understand and determine the standards of banking apps, the typical look and feel, and figure out how to improve and deliver better results.
